There have always been two contracts in open source: one for business, one for humans. As AI enters the fold, both as a technology and something being built on open source foundations, the human contract is at risk.

Join this call to talk through what it means to hold onto what openness has meant for justice, inclusion, and democracy for human outcomes. Share existing work, efforts, standards and communities already in this space. Help brainstorm what comes next.
