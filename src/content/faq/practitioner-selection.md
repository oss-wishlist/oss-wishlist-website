We carefully vet all practitioners to ensure quality matches:

1. **Application Review** - We evaluate experience, expertise, and past work in open source
2. **Matching Assessment** - We learn about preferences, availability, timezone, and goals
3. **Onboarding** - Approved practitioners attend onboarding and get a profile on our site
4. **Ongoing Evaluation** - Maintainers, sponsors, and practitioners can provide feedback

For our initial pilot, we're keeping things small with up to 10 practitioners while we refine the process. We'll expand from there based on demand and capacity.
