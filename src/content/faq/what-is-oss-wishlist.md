Open Source Wishlist is a grassroots community of practitioners connecting to drive impact through sustainability, openness in AI, digital sovereignty, and beyond while supporting each other along the way.

One of our key initiatives is OSS Wishlists - a structured way for maintainers to request specific help and for practitioners to fulfill those needs with clear deliverables and proven outcomes.
